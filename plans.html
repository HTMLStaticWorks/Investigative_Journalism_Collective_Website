Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about membership at The Collective.

Is my membership tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, and your monthly or annual contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can manage your subscription directly from your dashboard or contact our member support team at any time.

Is my payment secure?

All transactions are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant payment gateways. We never store your card details.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership tier at any point from your dashboard without losing any accrued benefits.
