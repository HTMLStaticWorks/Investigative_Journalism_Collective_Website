Our Mission

Democracy Dies in the Dark.

The Investigative Journalism Collective is a non-profit newsroom dedicated to unearthing the truth behind power structures and systemic failures.

Holding Power Accountable.

Since our founding in 2018, we have published over 500 deep-dive investigations that have led to legislative changes, corporate restructuring, and international sanctions.

Unlike traditional media outlets, we are 100% reader-funded. This means we answer to no one but the truth. Our journalists spend months, sometimes years, on a single story to ensure every fact is bulletproof.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values.

These four principles are the foundation upon which every story, every source, and every published word rests.

Source Protection

Whistleblowers and sources are the lifeblood of accountability. We protect identities with military-grade security and legal firepower.

Radical Independence

100% reader-funded. No advertisers, no corporate masters, no political affiliations. Our loyalty is to the public record — period.

Data-First Journalism

Every claim is supported by verifiable data, FOIA records, financial disclosures, or technical forensics. We don't speculate — we prove.

Global Accountability

Power doesn't respect borders. Our investigations span 30+ countries, ensuring no systemic failure is out of reach of the public eye.
